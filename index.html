About XLPower

            

                

                    
                

                

                    

                        XLPower was founded by **Seelam Hemanth**, a 4th-year Computer Science Engineering student at Vignan University. Driven by the desire to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry demands, Hemanth envisioned a platform that serves as a powerful ally for students preparing for their careers.
                    

                    

                        The platform's core purpose is to be a comprehensive, all-in-one resource. It provides tools for students to analyze and improve their resumes, sharpen their problem-solving skills through technical tests, and chart a personalized, data-driven learning path. XLPower is built on the belief that every student, with the right guidance and resources, has the potential to achieve their professional goals.
